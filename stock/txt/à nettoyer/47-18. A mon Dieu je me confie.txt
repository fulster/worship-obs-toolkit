47-18. A mon Dieu je me confie

1. A mon Dieu je me confie,
Sa clémence est infinie. 
Refrain
Ta pensée, ô Dieu d'amour,
Est fidèle, éternelle. 

2. La lumière radieuse
Est ton œuvre merveilleuse. Refr.

3. Tu consoles, tu pardonnes,
Ta bonté sur tous rayonne. Refr.

4. Par ta grâce prévenante
L'âme s'ouvre à l'espérance. Refr.

5. Tout à toi je me confie,
Ta clémence est infinie. Refr.

Suzanne Bidgrain 1879-1961
(c) Auteur (106a)

English
1. Let us gladly with one mind
Praise the Lord for He is kind :
Chorus
For His mercy shall endure
Ever faithful, ever sure. 

2. He has made the realms of space,
All things have their ordained place : Chorus

3. He created sky and sea,
Field and mountain, flower and tree : Chorus

4. Every creation, great and small,
God alone has made them all : Chorus

5. Then at least he fashioned man,
Crowning glory of his plan : Chorus

6. He has shaped our destiny,
Heaven for all eternity : Chorus

7. Glory then to God on high,
" Glory ! " let creation cry : Chorus

John Milton 1608-1674 / Michael Saward *1932
