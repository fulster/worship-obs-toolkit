56-01. Dans le désert Dieu a parlé
Go down, Moses

1. Dans le désert, Dieu a parlé :
Libère Israël !
Du milieu d’un buisson en feu :
Libère Israël !
Refrain
Descends, Moïse,
En Egypte descends !
Dis à Pharaon :
Libère Israël !

2. Car mon peuple est chargé de liens :
Libère Israël !
Opprimé par les Egyptiens :
Libère Israël ! Refr.

3. Je suis l’Eternel tout-puissant :
Libère Israël
Je briserai le joug pesant :
Libère Israël ! Refr.

Alfred Kuen *1921

English
1. When Israel was in Egypt’s land – / Let my people go !
oppressed so hard they could not stand – / Let my people go !
Chorus
Go down, Moses, / way down in Egypt’s land,
tell old Pharaoh : / Let my people go !

2. No more shall they in bondage toil – / Let my people go !
let them come out with Egypt’s spoil – / Let my people go ! Chorus

3. The Lord told Moses what to do – / Let my people go !
to lead the children of Israel through – / Let my people go ! Chorus

4. O come along, Moses, you’ll not get lost – / Let my people go !
stretch out your rod, and come across – / Let my people go ! Chorus

5. When they reached the other shore – / Let my people go !
they sang a song of triumph o’er – / Let my people go ! Chorus

6. So let us all from bondage flee – / Let my people go !
and Jesus, he will set us free – / Let my people go ! Chorus
